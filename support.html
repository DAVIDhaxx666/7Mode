Troubleshooting

Installation Issues

  • Verify that your system meets the system requirements.
  • Ensure that you have 8 GiBs of free space and 8 GB of RAM.
  • Double-check your download for completeness and integrity.

Performance Problems

  • Close any unnecessary applications running in the background.
  • Ensure your system drivers are up to date.

  • Refer to the User Manual for a list of common error messages and solutions.
